Installing an LED strip on a TV

Before placing the strips themselves, we pre-glue the LED strip, cut it in accordance with the dimensions previously prepared slats. In my case, I changed the standard tape of mediocre quality to a proven one I can't say whether in your case there will initially be good tape from 3M on the tape or bad, in any In this case, it will be possible to re-glue it in the future if the tape peels off from the set place.

I cut the aluminum bar into segments in accordance with the size of the TV. For the bar intended for the lower part gave the necessary profile to ensure a fit on the unevenness of the body.

To place the aluminum slats on the TV case, I purchased a textile tape fastener - Velcro. Cut off a few small segments and glued to aluminum slats. At the moment, I am satisfied with such a solution, it is possible In the future, if the slats sag, I will glue them directly to a good double-sided tape.
